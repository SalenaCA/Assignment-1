Sammy's Jewelry Store

Our Office

Our main office is located at 112 Molines Road Unit 2, Kingston Jamaica.

Sammy's Jewelry Store has grown to become one of the most recognized and respected jewelry stores in Jamaica. For more than 30 years, Sammy's Jewelry store has built its reputation by consistently providing exceptional quality at affordable prices, paired with superior service. The business is based on integrity, professionalism and attention to detail, resulting in long-standing, trusting relationships.

As a second generation family-owned business, our goal is to provide personal attention to each customer in a warm, inviting atmosphere. We continue to offer an extensive selection of classic jewelry pieces combined with on-trend styles. We currently have two locations, one in Kingston and the other in Montego Bay.
